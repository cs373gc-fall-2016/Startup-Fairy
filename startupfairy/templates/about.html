About Us

Startup Fairy is a guide for people who want to learn more about startup culture and get acquainted with the major players in the industry. Startup Fairy allows the user to search for statistics for a plethora of startups as well as company demographics that will help in their search for place of employment. The use of this service will allow for an aggregate foundation for data provided for a respective startup all in one coherent location.

The use of this site will provide for an easier transition into startup culture as well as understanding what each respective truly does as well as exhibit their company culture. There are various models implemented in the site to allow for users to search through different mediums to filter for what they are looking for in a company.
